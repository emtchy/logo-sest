# Typography

**Primary Typeface**

- JetBrains Mono Regular
- License: SIL Open Font License 1.1 (free for personal and commercial use)
- Download: https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/

**Fallbacks**

- Consolas, Courier New, monospace

**Usage**

- JetBrains Mono is the main typeface for the logotype and digital applications.
- It is particularly suitable for code-inspired, technical, and modern brand aesthetics.
- Fallback fonts are recommended when JetBrains Mono is not available.
